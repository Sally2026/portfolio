Global Solutions Institute

For our second client-based team project, and our Capstone project for the IT Minor, we partnered with the Global Solutions Institute to redesign and rebuild their website using WordPress. Our goal was to create a more organized, accessible, and visually engaging platform that clearly communicates the organization’s mission and programs. As the Co-Lead of the team, I helped guide project decisions, coordinate tasks, and maintain communication with the client. I also contributed to customizing WordPress themes, structuring pages, and enhancing the site’s usability and layout. This project strengthened my experience with leadership, WordPress development, and collaborative problem-solving while giving me hands-on practice creating a professional website for a real organization.

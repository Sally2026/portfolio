Argument Essay

This project involved researching and writing an argument essay on the topic of technological unemployment, exploring how automation and artificial intelligence impact the workforce. I developed a clear thesis, supported it with credible sources, and analyzed different perspectives on the economic and social effects of technology-driven job displacement. The essay required careful organization, logical reasoning, and persuasive writing to present a compelling argument. Through this project, I strengthened my research skills, critical thinking, and ability to communicate complex ideas effectively in writing.
